Content

Home page

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to shape a community for those people who enjoy riding a bike. If you like road cycling, track, mountains, BMX, bike-polo, or just going out for a tour, here can find a group to train with or just go to have fun with. Also will be the opportunity to subscribe to the website, and be ready to inscribe in every group and event.

People will come here to find a group and practice their favorite sport, riding a bike! They would find days and times to go to train or just go to ride, for every discipline. Will be available an option to subscribe to a group fast and simple. Also, they will find the last news about cycling.

my audience will choose my site over others because here can obtain all related to cycling in one place. This site offers you the opportunity to join a group, or rather, a community to practice your favorite sport. It is common for a cyclist to enjoy cycling in more than one discipline. For example, a track cyclist may enjoy riding on the road. A BMX rider may also enjoy riding in the mountains. So here you can find a group to practice each of the disciplines of the sport on a bike. You can upload your information such as where you live, what your availability hours are, and what disciplines you prefer to practice. We will help you find a group near you to go riding.

The information organization and the possibility to join the groups of all the country directly from this site will be the main distinction.

Benefits

The benefit of a site like this is to give a place to all those cycling teams that are distributed around the country. There are many groups, small and big, but sometimes they are not easy to find. On the other hand, some people would love to start practicing this sport, but they don't know how to do it or they don't have anyone to go out with. Here we could help those groups to be more visible and to reach more people, and anyone who wants to join a group to go out and practice this sport can find it.

Riding a bike opens doors such as better health, new friends, and new experiences. This project could help more people to find all these benefits, and they don't have to do it alone, there is a place to gather these people who share their likes for this sport.

Home Page organization

At the top of the page, the objective of the page is presented and a menu is displayed to choose what you want to do. First, what discipline you are interested in. Next, whether you want to join an informal or formal group.
