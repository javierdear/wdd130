Purpose

The purpose of this site is to build revenue by a market of brownies and cookies

Audience

My audience is made up of people from 18 to 40 years old, both workers and students. They are interested in consuming local market products, sharing with friends, and enjoying good times. They take time to rest and relax.

They want to find homemade, local, and quality food. They want to feel part of a community.

An unmet need is to find a homemade dessert or snack that is healthy and easy to obtain (available by delivery or local).

Mostly, my audience acces by movile device
